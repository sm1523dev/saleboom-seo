# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Marketers, SEO professionals, and growth-focused founders running small-to-mid-size businesses. They use this during working hours, on a desktop browser, in a focused task context — checking their site's ranking health or reviewing AI citation opportunities. They are results-oriented and will bounce on anything that wastes their time.

## Product Purpose

SaleBoom SEO is an AI-powered SEO + AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) platform that audits any website in under 60 seconds and gives actionable fixes. The core value: get your content cited by AI answer engines (Perplexity, ChatGPT, Gemini, Bing Copilot) in addition to traditional search ranking. Success looks like: user enters a URL, gets a scored audit, sees ranked fixes, pushes changes to their CMS directly.

## Brand Personality

Confident, intelligent, unfussy. The product feels like a sharp analyst, not a cheerful assistant. Three words: **precise, watchful, premium**.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS blue-and-white dashboards (anything resembling an older HubSpot or Moz UI)
- Loud, neon-accented "AI tool" aesthetics with heavy glow effects
- Bloated marketing landing pages with numbered section eyebrows, hero-metric dashboards, and identical icon-card grids
- Drop shadows that look like 2018 Material Design

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't shout** — use visual clarity over decorative effects; every element earns its space
2. **Trust through precision** — metrics, labels, and copy are factual and specific; no filler stats or clichéd AI copy
3. **Intelligence over decoration** — motion and color serve the product's analytical character, not the other way around
4. **Restrained brand** — tinted neutrals + one accent; let the content carry the weight

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigable. Reduced motion support (already configured in globals.css and GSAP matchMedia). Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 for body text, ≥ 3:1 for large text.
